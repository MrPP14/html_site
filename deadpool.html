The Deadpool




Deadpool is an antihero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.
Deadpool, whose real name is Wade Wilson, is a disfigured mercenary with the superhuman
ability of regeneration and physical prowess. The character is known as the "Merc with a Mouth"
because of his tendency to talk and joke constantly, including breaking the fourth wall for
humorous effect and running gags.
